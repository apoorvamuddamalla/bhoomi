HEALTH AND HYGIENE

HEALTH

* HEALTH IS DEFINED AS A STATE OF COMPLETE PHYSICAL, MENTAL AND SOCIAL-BEING

AND NOT MERELY AN ABSENCE OF DISEASE OR INFIRMITY.

* PHYSICAL HEALTH AND MENTAL HEALTH ARE INTER-RELATED.

* A SOUND MIND IN A SOUND BODY IS AN OLD AND APPROPRIATE SAYING FOR GOOD HEALTH.

* BEING HEALTHY SHOULD BE PART OF YOUR OVERALL LIFESTYLE.

* FEELING GOOD ABOUT YOURSELF AND TAKING CARE OF YOUR HEALTH

ARE IMPORTANT FOR YOUR SELF-ESTEEM AND SELF-IMAGE.

* MAINTAIN A HEALTHY LIFESTYLE BY DOING WHAT IS RIGHT

FOR YOUR BODY.
